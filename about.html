    ABOUT US

We are so glad you found us! Our mission is to assist those returning to work to develop a wardrobe that represents their style and comfort level. The WFR revolution shook up life as we know it. We traded our office business attire of dresses, suits, ties, uniforms, etc. for yoga pants, boxers, slippers, sneakers, t-shirts, and who knows what else.

Now, the hybrid schedule has arrived and it's time to go back to the office (at least for a couple of days). Most of us have evolved and changed in various ways. The wardrobe we used before has become stale and outdated. It may no longer reflect who we are now. We may have gained or lost weight so that those clothes don't fit anymore. But where to start?

Now there's Zoom 2 Boardroom Fashions. A revolutionary way to build a new wardrobe that reflects the new you, your values, your style, and your verve. Instead of endless hours of shopping in malls, outlet stores, and online, just answer a few questions, a few clicks, and voila!, Zoom 2 Boardroom Fashions will build you a wardrobe that is well, YOU.

You may wonder what sets Zoom 2 Boardroom Fashions apart from other similar companies. I am glad you asked! Zoom 2 Boardroom Fashions isn't just interested in the outside of you. It wants to represent you, the whole you. Why not have a wardrobe that is designed on your style, your interests, and your values. The others just focus on the outside, size, colors, and materials.

Let Zoom 2 Boardroom Fashions help you to discover and present the true you.
